Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Cal AI Tracker ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly: gender, date of birth, height, weight, fitness goals, and dietary preferences. This is used solely to generate your personalized nutrition plan.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used to calculate daily calorie and macro targets, personalize your experience, and improve app features. We do not sell your data to third parties.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using industry-standard encryption in transit and at rest.

4. Third-Party Services

We use Google Sign-In and Apple Sign-In for authentication. These services have their own privacy policies. We do not store your credentials.

5. Health Data

Cal AI Tracker may integrate with Apple Health and Google Fit to sync activity data. This data is only used to improve calorie recommendations and is never shared with third parties.

6. Children's Privacy

Cal AI Tracker is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

7. Your Rights

You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us.

8. Contact

For privacy questions: support@calaitracker.com
